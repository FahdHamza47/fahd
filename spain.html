Famous Landmarks

Casa Batlló

One of the most famous buildings in Barcelona, designed by Antoni Gaudí, known for its whimsical architecture.

Sagrada Familia

Designed by Antoni Gaudí, this is one of Spain's most iconic landmarks in Barcelona.

Parc Ciutadella

A beautiful park in Barcelona that is home to a zoo, museums, and a large lake.

La Boqueria

A popular market in Barcelona known for its fresh produce, meat, seafood, and local delicacies.

Spanish Cuisine

Paella

A famous rice dish from Valencia, usually made with seafood or chicken and served with saffron.

Patatas Bravas

Fried potato cubes served with a spicy tomato sauce, a staple tapas dish in Spain.

Spanish Pisto

A vegetable stew made with tomatoes, peppers, zucchini, and sometimes eggs, often served with bread.
